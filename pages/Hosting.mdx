
## Hosting Your **PageCraft** Site

With **PageCraft** as your static site generator, you have the flexibility to host your site on any static web hosting service. Here are some top choices:

- **[GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/)**: Seamlessly integrate with your GitHub repositories to host your site directly from your code.
- **[Netlify](https://www.netlify.com/)**: Enjoy automatic deployment, custom domains, and built-in SSL with an easy setup process.
- **[Vercel](https://vercel.com/)**: Deploy your site with exceptional speed and reliability, plus support for custom domains and instant rollbacks.
- **[Cloudflare Pages](https://pages.dev/)**: Benefit from Cloudflareâ€™s global CDN for fast and secure hosting with minimal configuration.

If you prefer a more personal touch, you can also share your site with friends and family by hosting it on cloud storage services like Google Drive or Dropbox.

> Make sure to rename your HTML to index.html!
